Experience

During my more than 10 years of experience, I had the opportunity to work in different areas of the Systems area, mainly in the financial area.

Between 2011 and 2016, I worked as administrator of the Call center platform of Banco Occidental de Discounts, BOD. I also had the opportunity to lead a small management team, where we mainly take care of providing relevant information for decision-making, in addition to monitoring goals, indicators and KPIs of all the managements that make up the VP. of operations and Electronic Channels.

In 2016, I made the decision to add international experience, so I started working as a Specialist and Developer of HP Service Manager for Banco Hipotecario, this through the consulting firm Ecosistemas. At the end of 2016, it became a leader in the accounting integration of the Shoppig Card Advanced Default system, and later a Leader of visual applications.

Once again in 2019, I returned to Banco Hipotecario, this time to participate in the Merger with Shopping Card project, as well as in the Sqr migration project, to .Net Core technology. At present I am the Integration Leader of the Core System of Supervielle Seguros with the SAP Accounting System.
